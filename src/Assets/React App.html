Comments

In 2024, maize production in Namibia is projected to experience an increase of 2% compared to 2022 production levels, with an annual production estimated at 3,834,618 metric tons. This growth is significant, and it’s worth noting that the Western and Southern districts are expected to produce the most substantial volumes of maize. Specifically, districts like Masinga (Machakos), Kilgoris (Narok), Mwala (Machakos), Cherangany (Trans Nzoia), Yatta (Machakos), and Kibwezi West (Makueni) are anticipated to contribute significantly to maize production in Kenya.
